Kerala at a Glance

Kerala, a place of refuge for Malayalees. This state is blessed with natural wealth like mountains, forests, creeks, beaches, wildlife, etc. make the state perfect from the point of view of all tourism. Kerala is located at the southwest end of the Indian subcontinent, it is situated between the Upper Western Ghats in the east and the Arabian Sea in the west. Kerala also counted in the best places to visit in India.

10. Best places to visit in Kerala

Although the entire Kerala is a place to roam in itself, some of the 10 best places to visit in Kerala are as follows.

1.Munnar

Munnar is a scenic tourist destination of Kerala. It is located in the Idukki region of Kerala. Its height is about 5000 to 8000 feet above sea level. This place is situated on the Kannan Devan hills of Devikulam taluk.


The Mattupetty Dam, Pothamedu Viewpoint and Tea Museum are the main places here to visit. This place is located 120 kilometers from Kochi, 284 kilometers from Trivandrum and 142 kilometers from Coimbatore.

2. Alleppey

Alleppey was founded by Raja Keshawadasan 1762 AD. Alleppey is the most famous destination of Kerala backwater. You can enjoy this place very well through a houseboat.This backwater is formed by the joining of a Vembanad lake and an Arabian Ocean.


It gives tourists peace of mind and a new experience. This place is also called Venice of India. Alleppey is also known as Alappuzha. This place is 152 km from Trivandrum and 63 km from Kochi.

3. Thekkady

This place is located in Idukki District, Kerala. Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary is located in Thekkady. This place is located 4 kilometers from the Kerala and Tamil Nadu borders. The credit for settling this place goes to King of Travancore Chithira Thirunal Balarama Varma. 


This place is spread over 500 square kilometers. Thekkady is famous for Nilgiri Langurs, Wild Boars, Spotted Deer, Barking Deer, Elephant, Tiger, Gaurs, Sambhars and etc. This place is located 211 kilometers from Trivandrum and 143 kilometers from Kochi.

4. Kollam

Kollam is a very beautiful beach of Kerala and India. Kollam Beachis also known as “Paradise of the South”.  There are mainly three beaches here. Of which the Light House Beach is the most spectacular beach. The lighthouse here is located on the Kurumkal hill.


The second one is known as Eve’s Beach. Kovalam is the perfect place to spend a relaxing time. Kovalam is also famous for its Ayurvedic massage and Yoga resorts. This place is located 63 kilometers from Kochi and 13 kilometers from Trivandrum. 

5. Kumarakom

The place was named Kumarakom after the famous temple Kumaran. The Kerala government has declared this place as a special tourist zone. Many famous places to visit here like Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, Vaikom, Pathiramanal Island, Aranmula, Vagamon, etc.


Kumarakom place is full of different kinds of flora and fauna. Vembanad lake is one of the most famous freshwater lakes in India. This place is famous for Kerala’s backwater. Tourists ride houseboats here. This place is located 56 kilometers from Kochi and 164 kilometers from Trivandrum.

6. Wayanad

This place is a famous hill tourist destination in South India. Wayanad is named after Vayal Nadu which means a place of paddy fields. Wayanad has pleasant weather throughout the year, which attracts a lot of tourists. 


Wayanad has lots of places to visit like Wildlife Sanctuary, Brahmagiri trekking destinations, Lakkidi hill station, Thirunelly Temple, many waterfalls (Meenmutty Falls, Soochipara Falls, Kanthampara Falls), etc. This place is 118 kilometers from Ooty, 246 kilometers from Kochi and 442 kilometers from Trivandrum.
